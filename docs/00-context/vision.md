# Vision

## Problem Statement
Public-sector approvals, policy changes, and procurement actions are spread across fragmented municipal meeting systems and difficult to monitor consistently.

## Outcome
CivicSignal provides daily, searchable intelligence from local government meetings by turning agendas and staff reports into structured signals that teams can act on.

## Non-Goals (Phase 1)
- Full ingestion of environmental impact reports, traffic studies, and large engineering appendices
- APN/address extraction as a required signal field
- Multi-region expansion beyond the initial 10 Orange County cities

## Stakeholders
- Product: defines taxonomy quality, search experience, and user workflows
- Engineering: owns connectors, ingestion pipeline, signal engine, and UI
- Operations: owns monitoring, scrape reliability, and incident response
- End users: analysts, business development, policy teams
